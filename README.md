# 📖 About

OrganStudy is a study management application focused on helping students build a consistent study routine.

Instead of simply organizing tasks, the application is based on **study hours**, allowing users to monitor how much time they invest in each subject and follow their progress over time.

The goal is to make study planning simple, visual, and motivating.

---

# ✨ Features

## 📚 Subject Management

Organize your study subjects in one place.

- Create subjects
- Edit subjects
- Delete subjects

---

## ⏱️ Pomodoro Timer

Stay focused using a built-in Pomodoro timer.

- Start/Pause timer
- Register study sessions
- Save study time automatically

---

## 📅 Calendar

Visualize your study routine.

- Daily study history
- Calendar view
- Planned future sessions

---

## 🎯 Weekly Goals

Set weekly study goals based on hours.

Examples:

- Study 20 hours this week
- Complete 8 hours of Algorithms
- Study Java for at least 5 hours

---

## 📊 Statistics

Track your progress through dashboards.

- Total study hours
- Hours per subject
- Weekly progress
- Monthly progress
- Productivity charts

---

## 🔥 Study Streak

Keep your consistency.

- Consecutive study days
- Weekly streak
- Longest streak
